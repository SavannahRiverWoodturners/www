Submit Meeting Minutes

Club members can submit meeting minutes for publication on the website.

Click here to submit meeting minutes

Instructions

  1. Click the link above
  2. Fill out the form with:
    • Meeting date
    • Your name
    • Meeting title
    • Meeting content (copy and paste from your document)
    • Drag and drop images, then add descriptions below each image
  3. Click “Submit new issue”
  4. You’ll receive an email when your post is approved or if changes are needed
