# Site Overview

## Purpose
The Savannah River Woodturners Club website serves three audiences:
- **Current members** — meeting schedule, meeting post write-ups with photos, officer info
- **Prospective members** — who we are, what we do, how to get involved
- **General public** — find local woodturners for projects, learn about the club

## Club Facts
- Founded: 2008
- Location: 144 North Belair Road, Evans, Georgia (CSRA region)
- Affiliated with the American Woodturners Association (AAW)
- Meetings: Saturdays at 9:00 am monthly; December is a Christmas Party at noon
- Contact: Mike Vernon, Secretary/Treasurer — 706-341-3999

## History
This site is a redesign of the original Jekyll-based site at
`savannahriverwoodturners.github.io` (repo: `SavannahRiverWoodturners/savannahriverwoodturners.github.io`).
That site used the Hydeout theme with a brown/rust color palette. This Hugo
version is a clean conversion of the same content with no theme selected yet.
